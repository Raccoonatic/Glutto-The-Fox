# 🦝✨ So Long — Glutto the Fox ✨🧡

> A neon-lit descent into hunger, guilt, and fragile redemption.

---

## 🎮 Description

**So Long** is a 2D game developed in **C** using the **MiniLibX (mlx)** graphics library, created as part of the 42 curriculum. At its core, the project focuses on map parsing, rendering, event handling, and building a simple game loop.

But this is not just a technical exercise.

---

### 🦊 Glutto the Fox — The Story

Glutto is a fox with a *mad case of the blues*.  
In his dreams, his guilt traps him inside a distorted version of reality—one where his hunger dictates everything.

Each room is a prison.  
Each door is locked.  
Each step forward is denied…  
until every last arepa has been devoured.

🌮 The arepas are not just food — they are compulsion.  
👻 The ghostly orange figures are not just enemies — they might be help, misunderstood.  
💔 And Glutto? He is not strong.

When the hunger takes over, it’s as if the world narrows.  
No exit exists until the room is empty.

And when those ghostly figures get too close…  
the stress alone might be enough to stop his fragile heart.

---

### 🎯 Goal

Guide Glutto through his looping dreamscape:

- 🟧 Collect **all arepas (C)**
- 🚪 Unlock the **exit (E)** only after everything is collected
- 👻 Avoid **ghostly enemies (X)**
- 💔 Survive long enough to escape

Will you help him confront his guilt…  
or let the fear consume him?
